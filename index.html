Clinical Specialist – Medical Technology (MR Technologist Background)

Full‑time United Arab Emirates (UAE)

About Klas‑Med: Klas‑Med is a leading provider of advanced AI‑based software and solutions across the UAE. Our mission is to transform healthcare through innovation, digital integration, and exceptional service.

Position Summary: Provide expert clinical support, training, and consultation on our portfolio of medical devices and digital solutions—bridging end‑users and technical teams.

Key Responsibilities

  • Provide clinical application support for Klas‑Med’s AI solutions.
  • Conduct product demonstrations, training sessions, and help in on‑site installations.
  • Act as the liaison between end‑users and technical/product teams.
  • Support the sales team by providing expert clinical insight during client engagements.
  • Assist with troubleshooting and post‑implementation follow‑ups.
  • Have knowledge of PACS, hospital IT systems, and clinical workflows.
  • Promote and support integration of AI‑powered tools in clinical settings.

Qualifications

  • Bachelor’s degree in Medical Technology or a related field.
  • 2–3 years of hands‑on clinical experience in a hospital or diagnostic setting is a must.
  • Prior experience as an MR Technologist is essential.
  • Proficient with hospital IT systems (PACS, RIS, HIS) and imaging workflows.
  • Excellent communication and interpersonal skills.
  • Technologically savvy with a strong interest in medical AI and innovation.
  • UAE residency or willingness to relocate preferred.

Desirable Additional Qualities

  • Experience conducting product or clinical training.
  • Knowledge of compliance and regulatory standards in medical imaging.
  • Multilingual (Arabic is a plus).
  • Ability to travel across the UAE for on‑site support.

To Apply: Email your CV and a brief cover letter to kalyan@klas-med.com.
